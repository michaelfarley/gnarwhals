North Coast Gnarwhals

Youth Mountain Bike Racing in Cleveland

For students in grades 5-12 of all ability levels, with seasons running from late May through early October

Who We Are

The North Coast Gnarwhals are the Cleveland area's only team affiliated with the National Interscholastic Cycling Association (NICA) and compete as part of the Ohio Interscholastic Cycling League. Open to students in grades 5-12, the Gnarwhals emphasize teamwork, personal growth, and the joy of ridingâ€”no tryouts, no cuts, just a welcoming environment where everyone rides. The Gnarwhals primarily practice at the Medina mountain bike trail system.

Practice Schedule & Locations

Our practices feature group rides and skill drills, creating a fun environment where kids can build friendships.

When We Practice

  • Days: Mondays and Thursdays
  • Season: Starting June 9th, 2025 and ending October 2nd, 2025
  • Times: 6:00PM to 7:30PM

Where We Practice

  • Mondays: Medina Mountain Bike Trail (Huffman Park), Medina, Ohio
  • Thursdays: Varying locations including Royalview and Bedford

Commitment Level

No practice commitment required. The more you can attend, the merrier!

Racing Opportunities

The season includes six events starting in June with races beginning in August. Races are optional. Student-athletes can race as much or as little as they like or simply enjoy team practices.

Equipment You'll Need

A bike, a helmet and a water bottle are all you need.

Mountain Bikes

No bike? No problem. You can borrow a Gnarwhals loaner bike for the season by emailing our head coach, or bring your own if you prefer.

Safety Gear & Accessories

The only other required gear is a helmet and a water bottle. If you don't have a helmet, please let us know and we'll track one down for you.

Program Costs

NICA and the North Coast Gnarwhals are 501(c)(3) non-profit organizations.

Pricing Options

  • Program Participation: $195
    Fee for participating in the program and includes ability to join any or all practices. Includes $40 team fee that goes directly to the Gnarwhals.
  • Race Package: $200
    Race fee for all events. Remember, racing is optional.
  • Try-it Program: $60
    For first year student athletes who aren't sure if racing is going to be their thing. $60 for one race with the option of 'buying up' to the full race package.
